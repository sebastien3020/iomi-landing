# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Founders, business operators, high-income professionals, real estate and crypto investors — people whose finances outgrew spreadsheets. They land on this page from a referral or a post, evaluating whether IOMI is serious enough to hold their full financial picture. Context: desktop first, evening research session, comparing against Kubera / Copilot / a private banker's PDF.

## Product Purpose

IOMI is an AI Wealth Operating System: one dashboard unifying personal + business assets (banks, brokerages, real estate, business equity, crypto, liabilities), with an intelligence layer (Capital Efficiency Score, recommendations, simulator, goals). This landing page's job: make a sophisticated buyer trust the product enough to start the free trial.

## Brand Personality

Confident, precise, decisive. A modern private bank, not a consumer app. Reference feel: Linear, Mercury, Ramp — sober, fast, exact motion; nothing gratuitous. Dark theme, single green brand accent (#26BC4D).

## Anti-references

- Gamified trading apps (Robinhood): no confetti, no dopamine loops, no playful bounce.
- Corporate banking sites: no navy-blue stock-photo distance, no institutional stiffness.
- Crypto-hype aesthetics: no neon gradients, no purple glow, no FOMO energy.

## Design Principles

1. **Show the product, not adjectives** — real mocked UI (dashboard, recommendations, goals) carries the argument.
2. **Motion proves precision** — animations are fast, eased-out, and always tied to data appearing (counters, bars, chart draw-ins). Never decorative loops.
3. **One accent, spent carefully** — green marks money-positive and interactive moments only.
4. **Numbers are the heroes** — net worth, CES, percentages get the visual priority.
5. **Trust through restraint** — disclaimers visible, claims specific, no hype vocabulary.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. `prefers-reduced-motion` honored on every animation (instant/crossfade fallback). Contrast ≥4.5:1 for body text on dark surfaces. No information carried by color alone (statuses also carry text labels).
