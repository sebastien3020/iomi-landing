🛡 Security & privacy

Your data, protected at every layer.

IOMI never sees your bank credentials — connections go through Plaid, trusted by leading financial apps. Every credential we do hold is encrypted at rest with AES-256, and all traffic is encrypted in transit over HTTPS/TLS.

AES-256 encryption

Bank tokens, API keys, and secrets are encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM — versioned and upgradeable.

Argon2id hashing

Your password is never stored — only hashed with Argon2id, the OWASP-recommended gold standard.

2FA & session alerts

Optional TOTP two-factor authentication (authenticator app) and new-device login alerts via email.

AI privacy by design

Account numbers, routing numbers, and personal identifiers are never sent to the AI model — only aggregated figures.

Full account numbers are never stored — only the last 4 digits for display. Rate-limited endpoints, parameterized queries, and strict access controls protect against unauthorized access. See the full security overview →
